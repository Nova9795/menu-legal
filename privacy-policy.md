# Privacy Policy

**App:** MenU  
**Last updated:** May 2026

## Overview

MenU is a private habit tracker for a small closed group of users. This policy explains what data the app collects, how it is used, and your rights regarding that data.

---

## Data We Collect

### Health & Fitness Data
- Menstrual cycle dates and phase information (HER profile only)
- Supplement names, dosages, and tracking records
- Daily habit completion records
- Supplement cycling schedules and progress

### Usage Data
- Habit completion history and streaks
- App interaction data used to display your progress calendar

### User Content
- Messages sent through the AI chat feature

### Device Identifiers
- Push notification token (if you enable notifications)

---

## How We Use Your Data

All data collected is used solely to provide the app's core functionality:

- Displaying your daily habit checklist and tracking completions
- Calculating supplement cycle phases and menstrual cycle predictions
- Generating your progress calendar and streak statistics
- Processing AI chat requests to answer questions about your protocol and edit your habits

We do not use your data for advertising, analytics, or any purpose beyond operating the app.

---

## Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using [Supabase](https://supabase.com), a cloud database provider. Data is stored in their hosted Postgres database and is encrypted in transit and at rest. Supabase's privacy policy is available at https://supabase.com/privacy.

---

## Third-Party Services

### OpenRouter / AI Chat
When you use the AI chat feature and have given your explicit consent, your habit data and chat messages are transmitted to OpenRouter (https://openrouter.ai) for AI processing. This includes:
- Your habit items, supplement stack, and current cycle phases
- The messages you type in the chat

OpenRouter's privacy policy is available at https://openrouter.ai/privacy. You can withdraw consent for AI features at any time from the Settings screen inside the app.

---

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties except as described above (Supabase for storage, OpenRouter for AI chat when consented).

---

## Data Linked to Identity

This app has no user accounts. No data collected by this app is linked to your identity.

---

## Data Retention

Your data remains in the database for as long as you use the app. There is no automatic deletion. If you wish to have your data deleted, contact us at the email below and we will delete it within 30 days.

---

## Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 12. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 12.

---

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected by the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
